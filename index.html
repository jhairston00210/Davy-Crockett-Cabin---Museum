The Davy Crockett Cabin in Rutherford, Tennessee was the last home of Davy Crockett.
Davy Crockett, the King of the Wild Frontier, was an American hero, frontiersman, folk hero, three-times congressman, and soldier. You can learn more about him and his home in the 'Biography' and 'History of the Cabin' pages respectively.
